Agra

The Taj City

Agra is a city on the banks of the river Yamuna in the northern state of Uttar Pradesh. Agra is one of the most populous cities in Uttar Pradesh, and the 24th most populous city in India.

Delhi

The Capital Of India

Delhi, India's capital territory, is a massive metropolitan area in the country's north.Delhi is of great historical significance as an important commercial, transport, and cultural hub, the political centre of India.

Jaypur

The Pink City

Jaipur is the capital of India's Rajasthan state. It evokes the royal family that once ruled the region and that, in 1727, founded what is now called the Old City, or “Pink City” for its trademark building color.

Gwaliar

The City Of Sindhiya's

Gwalior is a city in the central Indian state of Madhya Pradesh. It's known for its palaces and temples, including the Sas Bahu Ka Mandir, intricately carved Hindu temple and Ancient Gwalior Fort.

Mumbai

The LifeLine Of India

Mumbai (formerly called Bombay) is a densely populated city on India's west coast. A financial center, it's India' largest city The city's also famous as the heart of the Bollywood film industry.
